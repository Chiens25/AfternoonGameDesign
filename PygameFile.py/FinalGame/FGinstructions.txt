This game consists of a Level 1, 2, and 3
Level 1 is the easiest. You will play a game of Rock, Paper, Scissors
If you win, you will enter a maze. 
Use the arrow keys to reach the star and score a point.
Level 2 is the same as Level 1, except the maze is timed.
If you don't finish before time runs out, you forfeit the point.
Level 3 is the same as Level 2, except the maze has obstacles.
If you touch an obstacle, you forfeit the point.
